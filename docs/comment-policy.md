# Comment Policy

- Keep real-time comments short enough to read without losing the presentation.
- Prefer concrete reactions tied to the current sentence or slide.
- Do not attack the presenter or speculate about sensitive traits.
- Avoid repeating the same observation within 30 seconds.
- Route long explanations and low-confidence criticism to the final report.
- Discard comments after their expiration time or when their evidence is no longer current.
